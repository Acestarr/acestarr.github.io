概念

二项式反演其实就是利用容斥的思想处理一些通过求“至少或至多”来解决“恰好”的问题。

f(n)=∑i=0n(−1)i(ni)g(i)  ⟺  g(n)=∑i=0n(−1)i(ni)f(i)f(n)=∑i=0n(ni)g(i)  ⟺  g(n)=∑i=0n(−1)n−i(ni)f(i)f(n)=∑i=nm(ni)g(i)  ⟺  g(n)=∑i=nm(−1)i−n(ni)f(i)\begin{aligned} f(n)=\sum_{i=0}^n(-1)^i\binom n i g(i)&\iff g(n)=\sum_{i=0}^n(-1)^i\binom n i f(i) \\ f(n)=\sum_{i=0}^n\binom n i g(i)&\iff g(n)=\sum_{i=0}^n(-1)^{n-i}\binom n i f(i) \\ f(n)=\sum_{i=n}^m \binom n i g(i)&\iff g(n)=\sum_{i=n}^m(-1)^{i-n}\binom n i f(i) \end{aligned} f(n)=i=0∑n​(−1)i(in​)g(i)f(n)=i=0∑n​(in​)g(i)f(n)=i=n∑m​(in​)g(i)​⟺g(n)=i=0∑n​(−1)i(in​)f(i)⟺g(n)=i=0∑n​(−1)n−i(in​)f(i)⟺g(n)=i=n∑m​(−1)i−n(in​)f(i)​
